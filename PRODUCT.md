# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Young Chinese content creators (18-35) who want to post on Xiaohongshu but struggle with writing polished copy. They speak naturally into their phone and want AI to transform their casual voice into engaging posts. Context: mobile-first, casual, personal moments.

## Product Purpose

Voice-to-post pipeline: user speaks casually about an experience, AI structures the content and generates a complete Xiaohongshu post (title, cover text, body, hashtags, comment prompt) in 5 selectable styles. Success = the user feels "this sounds exactly like me, just better organized."

## Brand Personality

Warm, relaxed, genuine. Like a close friend helping you organize your thoughts, not a professional copywriter. The interface should feel inviting, low-pressure, and personal.

## Anti-references

- SaaS dashboards with data-heavy layouts
- Marketing-heavy corporate tools
- Cold, technical AI interfaces with terminal aesthetics
- Over-designed portfolios with heavy animation

## Design Principles

1. **Speak, don't type.** The primary input is voice; the UI should encourage talking over typing.
2. **Your voice, amplified.** Every generated post must feel personal, not templated. Show the user's original words alongside the polished version.
3. **Low friction, high trust.** Minimize steps between thought and post. Never make the user feel like they're "using a tool."
4. **Small-screen native.** Designed for phone-first usage patterns, even on desktop.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Support reduced motion. Chinese-optimized typography. High contrast text on all backgrounds.
